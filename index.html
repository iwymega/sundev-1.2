About Tour

Embark on an unforgettable sunrise adventure with SUNDEV trekking! This thrilling trek takes you to the summit of the active volcano, Mount Batur.

Your Journey
  • Early Start: We begin before dawn, picking you up directly from your hotel and delivering you to the starting point.
  • Fuel Up: Enjoy a light breakfast of banana pancakes and tea/coffee before your ascent.
  • Ascent to the Peak: Embark on a roughly 2-hour climb guided by our experienced team, reaching the summit around 4:00 AM.
  • Sunrise Spectacle: Take a short break at the “Stop will be made” point to refresh with water and witness the breathtaking sunrise.
  • Volcanic Breakfast: Savor a unique breakfast of pre-packed food and eggs cooked in the volcanic steam while enjoying the panoramic views.
  • Crater Exploration: Descend the volcano, exploring the three younger craters and the most recent lava flows on your way down.
  • Journey’s End: Arrive at the finish point by 9:10 AM and board the transport back to your hotel. (Optional stops at Bali Swing, coffee plantations, and rice terraces are available)
